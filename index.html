Birds of Peramagroon

This guide contains information on the 133 species known from Peramagroon Mountain. It is intended as an easy and quick way to identify birds found here.

The Birds of the Middle East book should be consulted for more detailed information on these species or for further help with difficult identifications. Tap the image below to purchase a copy of the book.

Many of the same birds will be found in similar habitats nearby. This app can therefore be used in other places â€“ but with care.

How to use:

  1. Navigate between screens using the buttons at the top left and right of the screen.
  2. Tap a bird in the list to see a description.
  3. Set a filter to order the species list by relevance.

Abbreviations:

  • ad - adult
  • juv - juvenile
  • ♀ - female
  • ♂ - male

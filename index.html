Birds of Peramagroon

This guide is intended to help in the identification of the birds of Peramagroon Mountain.

The Birds of the Middle East book should be consulted for more detailed information.

How to use:

Navigate between screens using the buttons at the top left and right of the screen. Use the 'filter' selection to choose your characters.

Abbreviations used in the guide

  • ad - adult
  • juv - juvenile 
  • ♀  - female
  • ♂  - male
